“Apples of Mars” is a mobile game for mono raw eaters, which will turn a routine food system into a game.

About the game

Humanity populates Mars. It turned out that the atmosphere of Mars allows you to eat one product without mixing it with others, otherwise the destruction of the body begins. In such a situation, it is possible to survive only thanks to the mono-meal raw diet.

Who is the game for?

For those who want to try mono-meal raw diet, but are not confident in themselves.

For those who have had the experience of mono-meal raw diet and are ready to repeat it.

For those who are looking for like-minded people in mono-meal raw diet.

Game mechanics

You are creating an account. You have landed on Mars.

You expose the period of your mono-meal raw diet - this is your life force.

You record every day spent on Mars in MONO mode and track how your life force grows.

You fill out the calendar: build a diet and reminders. You make new friends.

You write a blog about your successes and diet. Commenting on friends.

You get to the end of the diet. And you leave Mars. Mission Complete!

Life force

Combine events offline and online. One day in mono-meal raw diet - one point. Set your goal and move towards it. How many days you will be on Mars - you decide.
